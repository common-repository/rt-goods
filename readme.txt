== Description ==

Redtram Goods Informer is an ad unit that consists of an image, brief description and price of a product or service. This kind of advertising has a high click cost and profitability.
We help you to customize the appearance of commodity informers to optimally fit them into the design of your website. Our large goods catalog allows you to choose the products that will be most suitable for visitors to your WEB site and increase your profits as result. Show ads on your WEB site  that according to the interests of your audience and get paid for the targeted clicks of your visitors.

The service uses websocket technology.
The service executes requests to the company addresses (redtram.com, grt01.com, grt03.com) to get view and list of advertised products.

== Installation ==

1. Upload RT_Goods to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Register in Redtram cabinet https://my.redtram.com/
3. Create goods ticker
4. Add widget into page
